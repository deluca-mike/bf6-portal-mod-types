    IsFiring

    IsFiring: number

    Whether the soldier is firing. It is true for as long as it takes the weapon to animate firing, no matter how hard or long you press the trigger. If you rapidly fire semi-auto, it is true for every tick until you stop or run out of ammo. Trying to fire with no ammo does not result in this ever going true. Applies to anything (i.e. defibrillator, throwables, deployables), but not melee.
